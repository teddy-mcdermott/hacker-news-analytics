Hacker News Trends

Explore keyword popularity on Hacker News. This tool produces a graph comparing your search terms, so you can final prove to everyone you were right: pizza is more popular than burgers. I made this tool to use term frequency as a proxy for programming language trends, but I think you could seriously use it for other technological topics that are commonly spoken about on Hacker News.

Note: Search results are cached. High-frequency terms (like function words) may take longer to process on the first run.
